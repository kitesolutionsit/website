Training

The competition around you, now or in future will duplicate your accomplishments; a competitor is always aiming at you. The only way to stay ahead in this never-ending race is to build on technical skills, quality, drive, enthusiasm and leadership ability.

Design engineers of today must have a solid grasp of new subjects, and be able to respond quickly to new demands in creativity. This requires a broad-based range of skills and in-depth knowledge of CAD package, at SAI CONSULTANCY we stress upon in-depth learning of different modules of CAD software which enables deeper understanding, flexibility and constrains involved using the software.

Our Training Centre is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and training curriculum, training sessions are conducted by experienced professionals, the courses are specifically designed and updated by industrial experts keeping in mind the requirements of ever changing industry, we lay stress on both theoretical and practical aspects, with significant time allocated for practice. A dedicated placement cell will assist students and working professionals aspiring for a new job.

Our training programs are flexible; we offer full-time and part-time programs. If your needs are not catered by one of our standard courses, we will create tailor made programs to meet your exact requirement.

We conduct training programs for students, working professionals, corporate and customers (at their locations).
